I am a UX designer and researcher with a deep focus on both Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). My work centers on designing meaningful and intuitive interactions that seamlessly integrate technology into everyday life, enhancing user experiences in subtle yet impactful ways.

I have extensive experience across the entire design process—from user research and prototype development to user evaluations—having successfully executed these phases multiple times. My background in interaction design, robotics, and HCI enables me to bridge the gap between technical innovation and human-centered solutions.
